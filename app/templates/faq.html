General Questions

ThutoPele EduLearn is an online learning platform that offers courses in Programming, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Networking, and Cyber Security. We provide quality education to learners worldwide, from beginners to advanced professionals.

Getting started is easy! Simply create a free account, browse our course catalog, and enroll in any course that interests you. Most beginner courses are free to get you started on your learning journey.

We offer both free and paid courses. Many beginner-level courses are completely free. Paid courses provide advanced content, projects, and certificates of completion. We also have subscription plans for unlimited access to our course library.

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices. You can access courses on your smartphone, tablet, or computer. We're also developing mobile apps for iOS and Android for an even better mobile experience.

Account & Billing

Click the "Register" button in the top right corner. You can sign up as a student directly. If you're interested in becoming an instructor, you'll need an invitation code from our admin team.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for South African students. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid courses. If you're not satisfied with a course, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link expires after 24 hours for security.
