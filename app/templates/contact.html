Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply create a free account, browse our courses, and click "Enroll" on any course you're interested in. Most beginner courses are free!

Are the courses certified?

Yes! Upon completion of any paid course, you'll receive a verified certificate that you can share on LinkedIn and your resume.

Can I become an instructor?

We're always looking for expert instructors! Visit our "Become an Instructor" page to learn more about the application process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for South African students.
